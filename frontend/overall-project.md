1. Project Overview:
   Design a Multi-platform Mini-ERP system (Web & Mobile) tailored for small business owners and individual retailers who are non-tech-savvy. The core value is simplifying complex business operations through an AI Voice Assistant and OCR scanning.

2. Design Philosophy:

Simplicity First: Eliminate the complexity of traditional ERPs. Minimal steps for every action.

High Accessibility: Standard typography, high-contrast colors, and fully responsive design.

Human-in-the-Loop: AI never performs destructive actions (like saving to DB) directly. It generates a "Draft/Pending" state for the user to review and "Confirm" manually.

3. Core Business Pillars:

Inventory: Real-time stock tracking with AI low-stock alerts.

Orders: Inbound (via OCR invoice scanning) and Outbound (via Voice commands).

Cashflow: Simple revenue/expense tracking and basic debt management.

Analytics: Visual dashboards for profit/loss and top-selling products.

4. Key AI Interactions to Support:

Voice-to-Action: Converting natural speech (e.g., "Sell 5 cans of soda") into a structured order form.

OCR-to-Data: Extracting table data from photos of paper invoices into the system.

Smart Querying: Answering business questions (e.g., "How much did I make today?") via a natural language interface.

5. Visual Identity:

Primary Color: Deep Trust Blue (Professional/Reliable).

Secondary Colors: Success Green (Money in/Save), Alert Red (Low stock/Expense).

UI Style: Clean, Modern, using "Enterprise-class" components (similar to Ant Design style).
